About nowoi.com

Your Source for Free Online Tools

Welcome to nowoi.com –a free online tools platform built to provide simple, fast, and secure web utilities for everyone.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make essential developer and text tools accessible without installation, registration, or hidden fees. All tools work directly in your browser for maximum privacy and convenience.

What We Offer

  • Free online developer tools (JSON formatter, regex tester, minifiers)
  • Text processing utilities (word counter, case converter, slug generator)
  • Encoding and conversion tools (Base64, URL encoder)
  • Calculation tools (age calculator, unit converter, percentage calculator)
  • Secure and privacy-friendly usage

Our Principles

  • Completely Free Forever –No hidden fees, no premium tiers
  • No Account Required –Use all tools instantly
  • No Data Storage –Everything processes in your browser
  • Fast and Lightweight –Optimized for speed
  • Accessible Design –Works on all devices

Privacy First

We take your privacy seriously. All our tools process data locally in your browser –your data never leaves your device. We don't use cookies for tracking, and we don't collect personal information.

This website is independently operated and continuously improved to ensure performance, reliability, and usability.

If you have suggestions or feedback, feel free to contact us.
