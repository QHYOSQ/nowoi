Calculate your age in years, months, and days. 100% free. No sign-up required.

How to Use This Age Calculator

  1. Enter your birth date: Input your birth year, month, and day.
  2. Enter current date: Input the current year, month, and day (or any other date you want to calculate your age up to).
  3. Calculate age: Click the "Calculate Age" button to see your age in years, months, and days.
  4. View results: The calculated age will be displayed in the result area.

Step-by-Step Tutorial

Calculating Your Age

1. Open the Age Calculator tool

2. Enter your birth year (4 digits), then automatically move to month input

3. Enter your birth month (1-12), then automatically move to day input

4. Enter your birth day (1-31, valid for the selected month)

5. Repeat for the current date

6. Click the "Calculate Age" button

7. Your age will be displayed in years, months, and days

8. Use this information for various purposes like applying for jobs, insurance, or just for fun
