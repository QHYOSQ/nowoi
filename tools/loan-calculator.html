Step-by-Step Tutorial

Calculating Loan Payments

1. Open the Loan Calculator tool

2. Enter the loan amount in the "Loan Amount" field (e.g., 100000 for a $100,000 loan)

3. Enter the annual interest rate in the "Annual Interest Rate" field (e.g., 4.5 for 4.5%)

4. Enter the loan term in the "Loan Term" field (e.g., 30 for a 30-year loan)

5. Click the "Calculate" button

6. The result will show the monthly payment and total interest paid over the life of the loan

7. Use this information to compare different loan options
