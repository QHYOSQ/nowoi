FAQ

Is this word counter free?

Yes, it is completely free with no hidden fees. You can use it as often as you need without any limitations.

Does this tool store my text?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.

What counts as a word?

The tool counts any sequence of characters separated by spaces as a word. This includes numbers and hyphenated words.

Can I use this tool offline?

Yes. Once the page is loaded, you can use the word counter offline as all processing happens in your browser.
